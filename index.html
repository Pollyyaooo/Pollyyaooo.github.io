Hi! I am currently a first-year MSTI student at the University of Washington.

I am currently exploring research in embodied and physical AI, with a particular focus on multimodal machine learning and sensing-based systems.

If you are interested in collaboration or discussing shared research directions, please feel free to reach out.


I received my undergraduate training in landscape planning at East China University of Science and Technology, where I developed a foundation in spatial thinking, environmental systems, and human–environment interaction.

At the graduate level, I have built an interdisciplinary background in connected devices, spanning hardware and software technologies, design practices, and business perspectives.
